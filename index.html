Intro

My name is Natalie Colonna and I am a Customer Success Manager in New York City.

I currently work for a MarTech company, Impact Inc. We help Brands grow their Partnership Programs through automation tools.

While we are currently working from home, I've missed the view from our office in the Empire State Building.

Since I've gained extra time in the day from no longer needing to commute, I've decided to use that time to build some new skills. I'm currently working through the Bloc Web Developer course and hope to gain a solid foundation in full stack development.

About Me

I've always had an interest in web development ever since I took my first HTML class in 5th grade. Unfortunately, I've never pursused that interest until now.

I love the combination of techincal and creative skills that are needed to complete a project.

Knowing that there is such a wide range of skills needed to complete a web development project, I look forward to working on a team of developers and collaborating closely with others.

After graduating Bloc I hope to be able to:

  • Communicate with technical resources internally and externally
  • Understand how full stack development can impact business initiatives
  • Provide technical solutions to business problems
  • Assist in scoping out required capabilities for business needs

When I'm not working or studying I love to be outdoors. My new puppy, Luna and I can always be found exploring a new park, trail, or canal on a sunny Saturday afternoon!
